About me

Building ideas that scale into real products.

I'm a Full Stack Developer passionate about turning ideas into polished, functional products. I specialize in the JavaScript ecosystem — React frontends, Firebase backends — and I'm equally at home in Python for data, automation, and quantitative work.

When I'm not coding, I'm thinking about user experience, game mechanics, and how technology can bring people together. My flagship project, La Gran Poya, is proof of that — a prediction platform built for the FIFA World Cup 2026 that brings groups of friends together around real competition.

Beyond products for people, I also build tools for myself — a quantitative trading stack for a nuclear-stocks portfolio, an AI-driven LinkedIn autoposter, and reusable scaffolding I reach for in every new project. Always something shipping.
